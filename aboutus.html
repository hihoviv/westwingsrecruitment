About Us

Empowering International Students for Success in New Zealand

Who We Are

West Wings is an international education consultancy dedicated to providing expert guidance and university placement services in New Zealand. Our mission is to empower students from around the world to achieve their academic and career goals by making the transition to studying in New Zealand as smooth and successful as possible.

Our Journey

Our story is rooted in personal experience. Having made the journey from India as international students ourselves, we understand the challenges, hopes, and dreams that come with moving to a new country. From navigating university applications and visa processes to adapting to a new culture and building a fulfilling life, we have lived every step of the journey we now help others take.

What We Offer

  • Personalized University Placement: We help you find the right university and program in New Zealand based on your background, interests, and aspirations.
  • Visa & Documentation Support: Our team guides you through the entire visa application process, ensuring all paperwork is accurate and stress-free.
  • Pre-Departure Preparation: We provide resources and briefings to help you feel confident and ready for your new adventure.
  • Ongoing Support: Our commitment doesn’t end when you arrive. We’re here to help you settle in, connect with the community, and thrive throughout your studies.

Our Commitment

We are passionate about supporting students in realizing their academic aspirations and long-term goals. Our firsthand experience means we offer not just professional expertise, but genuine empathy and understanding. At West Wings, your success is our success.
